In recent years, we have seen how the electric bikes became popular in the market and became a good option for reducing traffic, environmental protection and helping to endure for difficult journeys as well.
The main point for the electric bikes, is the price. They are obviously more expensive than a regular bike. But we may see developments for attractive options that make any bike an electric vehicle instantly.
ShareRoller, it is how we know this attractive project created in early 2014, but today, in its third version, has been perfected to the point of being ready to be marketed in bulk.
ShareRoller is a device placed in front of any bike through a pull-down mechanism. This small engine is capable of providing up to 750 watts of power, giving speeds of up to 45 km / h and an autonomy which can range from 12 to 89 kilometers, depending on the battery.
There are three types of batteries to choose producing 110, 220 or 400 watts of power per hour. One of the advantages is that these small engines are removable, so you can enlarge or diminish the autonomy (and weight) according to your needs. The battery is recharged in  from two hours to two and a half hours depending from the capacity of the engine.
ShareRoller also has a software that can be programmed from an iOS or Android smartphone via a mobile app , where we can check the autonomy time and distance traveled.
As an additional point, ShareRoller is portable , and can be removed from the bike and carried in a backpack for safety. Weight depends on the type of battery installed, but can range from 800 grams to almost 2 kilograms. Finally, as part of the potability, it has a USB device port to charge your mobile phone.




The entry package that includes a mini battery has a price of $ 649 , while the ultra battery version is available for $ 948.
